The Process

1. Start by envisioning your custom design. Once you have your concept, schedule an appointment with our expert jewelry designer, who will bring your idea to life. At this stage, we will prepare mock-up designs and make changes to a 3D CAD Model until you are satisfied.

2. Once a design is finalized, our expert gem cutters and goldsmiths utilize the highest quality of gemstones and fine metals to bring your piece to life through our custom fabrication process.

3. At this step, the production of your custom piece is now complete. For long distance clients, we will share photos and tracking information, and ensure the piece is delivered in a timely manner.
